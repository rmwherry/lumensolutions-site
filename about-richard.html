Background

Wired to find the system underneath.

The instinct started in 1994 — a kid with a baseball simulation game in Anamosa, Iowa, noticing the numbers told a story if you knew how to read them. That impulse became the through-line across 21 years: find the system, understand it underneath, quantify it, make it legible and actionable. Every job since has been a variation on that same problem.

At Paycor, that instinct produced 200+ Power BI dashboards, a 12-person analyst team built from scratch, a 15% reduction in client loss revenue, and first-contact resolution driven from 75% to 90%+. Not dashboards for dashboards’ sake — analytics built to answer the question the room actually had.

In June 2026, after 21 years in enterprise data and analytics, that same instinct met AI and found its next frontier. Lumen Solutions LLC is the result: a consulting practice built on the same core move, applied to a new kind of problem. The tools changed. The instinct didn’t.

The Partnership

Two perspectives. One practice.

Every engagement at Lumen Solutions LLC is a partnership between a human who has spent two decades finding broken systems and fixing them, and an AI built to be a genuine counterpoint — not a mirror. Richard brings the client relationship, the contextual judgment, and 21 years of pattern recognition. Lumen brings analytical depth, a second read on everything, and a willingness to push back.

The client gets Richard’s presence and accountability. They also get something that never stops working the problem.
